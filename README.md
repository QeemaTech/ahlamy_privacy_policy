# ahlamy_privacy_policy

**Last Updated:** February 24, 2026

---

## Introduction

Welcome to **Ahlamy**, the Islamic dream interpretation platform.
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.
This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Full name
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Account type (Dreamer / Interpreter)

### 1.2 Dream Data
- The text of dreams you submit for interpretation
- Dream details and dates
- Interpretations provided by certified interpreters

### 1.3 Usage Data
- Pages and sections you visit within the app
- Date and time of usage
- Device type and operating system

### 1.4 Location Data
- Location is used solely to determine the Qibla direction and prayer times
- Location data is not stored on our servers

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- To provide the dream interpretation service and connect dreamers with suitable interpreters
- To accurately display prayer times and Qibla direction
- To improve the user experience within the app
- To send notifications related to your dream's status (accepted, interpreted, etc.)
- To communicate with you regarding your account or inquiries

---

## 3. Information Sharing

We **do not sell** your personal data to any third party.

We may share your information only in the following cases:
- **With certified interpreters:** The interpreter assigned to your dream can view the dream text only, without access to your other personal details.
- **As required by law:** If required by a court order or legal obligation.
- **To protect rights:** To protect the rights and safety of users and the app.

---

## 4. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data, including:
- Encrypting passwords using strong hashing algorithms
- Using HTTPS protocol for all communications
- Restricting employee access to personal data
- Regular reviews of security procedures

---

## 5. Data Retention

- We retain your account data for as long as your account is active
- You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time
- After account deletion, we may retain certain data for a limited period for legal or security purposes

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data held by us
- **Correction:** Correct any inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data
- **Objection:** Object to the processing of your data for certain purposes
- **Unsubscribe:** Opt out of notifications at any time

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

**Ahlamy** is not directed at children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with their data, we will delete it immediately.

---

## 8. External Links

The app may contain links to external websites (such as Quran.com).
We are not responsible for the privacy policies of those websites.
We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external site you visit.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
We will notify you of any material changes via:
- An in-app notification
- Your registered email address

Your continued use of the app after changes are published constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

---

## 10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us through:

- **Email:** Via the "Email" section in the app's side menu
- **Customer Support:** Via the "Customer Service" section within the app
- **Website:** https://ahlamy.developteam.site/

---
